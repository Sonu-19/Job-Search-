Business Analyst

A Business Analyst is responsible for analyzing business processes and requirements to identify opportunities for improvement and provide data-driven solutions. They play a key role in facilitating communication between stakeholders and development teams.

Key Responsibilities:

Business Process Analysis
Requirement Gathering
Data Analysis
Solution Design
Stakeholder Communication
Business Impact Assessment
Project Documentation
Continuous Process Improvement

Skills and Qualities:

Data Analysis Tools (e.g., Excel, SQL)
Communication
Problem-Solving
Business Process Modeling
Requirement Elicitation
Attention to Detail
Adaptability
Teamwork

Specializations:

Business Analysts may specialize in areas such as data analysis, process improvement, or IT business analysis. Specializations allow them to focus on specific aspects of business analysis based on organizational needs.
